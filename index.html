Latest Renewable Energy Generation Technique

Solar Panels

Solar panels are a type of renewable energy source that convert sunlight into electrical energy. They are made up of photovoltaic (PV) cells, which convert light into electricity. Solar panels can be mounted on rooftops or in solar fields, and can be used to power homes, businesses, and even entire communities.

Wind Turbines

A wind turbine is a device that converts kinetic energy from the wind into electrical energy. It consists of blades attached to a central hub, which is connected to a rotor and an electrical generator.

Hydropower Plants

Hydroelectricity is generated by using the force of water flowing downhill to spin a turbine, which is connected to a generator that produces electricity.
